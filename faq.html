Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and mobile payment apps like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

How long will it take to get my vehicle detailed?

It varies greatly depending on the vehicle size, condition and package. Generally, The Outside (our exterior package) takes 1.5-2 hours. The Inside (interior package) takes 2.5-3 hours. And The Inside-Out takes about 4-5 hours.

Do you offer mobile detailing services?

Yes, we offer limited mobile detailing services to Markham residents and neighbouring cities such as Scarborough, Pickering, and Stouffville, with pricing calculated based on distance, so please give us a call to discuss and determine the price for your specific location.

Do you offer subscription plans?

Yes, we offer subscriptions for our detailing services, providing tailored plans to meet your vehicle's regular maintenance and care needs. Give us a call to explore our subscription options in more detail and find the best fit for you!
