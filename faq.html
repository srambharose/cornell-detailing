Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), and mobile payment apps like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

How long will it take to get my vehicle detailed?

It varies greatly depending on the vehicle size, condition and package. Generally, The Outside (our exterior package) takes 1.5-2 hours. The Inside (interior package) takes 2.5-3 hours. And The Inside-Out takes about 4-5 hours.

Do you offer mobile detailing services?

Yes, we offer limited mobile detailing services to Markham residents and neighbouring cities such as Scarborough, Pickering, and Stouffville, with pricing calculated based on distance, so please give us a call to discuss and determine the price for your specific location.

Do you offer subscription plans?

Yes, we offer subscriptions for our detailing services, providing a convenient solution to keep your vehicle in top-notch condition for the long haul. Subscribing to our services ensures that your car receives consistent and expert attention, maintaining its pristine appearance. Whether you're an Uber driver looking to impress your passengers with a spotless ride or a discerning car enthusiast who values the utmost care for your prized possession, our subscription plans are the perfect solution. It's essential to note that while subscriptions offer incredible value and convenience, they are not eligible for any ongoing promotions or discounts. However, the quality and personalized care you receive with a subscription make it an unbeatable investment for those who prioritize their vehicle's upkeep and appearance. Give us a call to explore our subscription options in more detail and find the best fit for you! Our team will be delighted to discuss the benefits and perks of our subscription plans, ensuring your car always stays in its prime condition.

Do you offer multi-vehicle discounts?

Yes, we value your loyalty and offer fantastic multi-vehicle discounts! When you bring us two vehicles for detailing, you'll enjoy a special discount of $10 off each vehicle's detailing service. This is the perfect opportunity for you and your spouse or partner to treat your cars to the royal treatment. Alternatively, you can team up with a neighbor and both receive $10 off each vehicle, making it a win-win for everyone in the community. Best of all, this multi-vehicle discount stacks with any ongoing promotions we have, allowing you to maximize your savings while ensuring your cars receive the care they deserve. And that's not all! If you bring us three vehicles for detailing, you'll still receive $10 off each vehicle's service. So, whether you have a fleet of cars or want to join forces with friends and family, our multi-vehicle discount ensures you get the same great savings on every vehicle.

Do you offer any other deals or discounts?

Birthday Month Special: Celebrate your birthday month with a delightful treat! Get $10 off any detailing service during your special month. Simply show us your ID as proof when booking your appointment, and this exclusive discount is yours. Better yet, this offer stacks with any other ongoing promotions, giving you double the reason to pamper your car while saving.
